## What & Why
The isolated V3 backtest engine now exists in the backend, but it is not exposed anywhere in the UI, so it is not usable from the app.

Also, the Railway deployment is currently failing on startup with:

TypeError [ERR_INVALID_ARG_TYPE]: The "path" argument must be of type string or an instance of URL. Received undefined
at fileURLToPath (node:internal/url:1606:11)

That means some API-server runtime path resolution is using `fileURLToPath(...)` with an undefined value after bundling to CommonJS.

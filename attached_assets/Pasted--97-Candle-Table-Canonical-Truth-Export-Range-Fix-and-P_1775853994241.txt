## PART 2 — Profit-Optimized Research Engine

### 9. Build deterministic strategy extractor from real candle truth
Using real candle data from `candles`, excluding interpolated rows by default, build a deterministic extractor that detects:

- spike events
- move clusters
- swing structures
- exhaustion/recovery sequences
- consolidation → expansion patterns
- medium/long-hold opportunity windows
- higher-frequency opportunities if the data supports them

This extractor must derive its findings from data, not from hardcoded examples.

---

### 10. Generate candidate strategies from observed data
Automatically derive candidate strategies such as:
- long-hold strategies
- medium-hold strategies
- higher-frequency strategies
- spike cluster recovery
- mean reversion
- continuation
- breakout
- exhaustion
- other detected families

Do not assume the correct answer is only 50-200% moves.
The system must optimize based on what the actual data supports.

---

### 11. For each candidate strategy compute profitability metrics
For each strategy candidate compute:
- trades per month
- avg return %
- median return %
- win rate
- avg hold duration
- max drawdown
- average capital lock time
- overlap / concurrency profile
- rough capital reuse efficiency
- expected monthly return on allocated capital
- estimated monthly contribution to total portfolio return

This must move the system from descriptive research to capital-extraction optimization.

---

### 12. Rank strategies by monthly profit potential
Rank the candidate strategies by:
- highest expected monthly return
- highest risk-adjusted monthly return
- best consistency
- best capital efficiency

The point is not “biggest move.”
The point is:
- highest conviction
- highest realistic win probability
- strongest monthly cash-flow extraction

---

### 13. AI becomes explainer, not inventor
The deterministic extractor computes the quantitative truth first.
The AI layer then:
- explains the top-ranked strategies
- describes their behavior
- highlights their risks
- compares them to current engine logic
- identifies whether current engines are aligned or misaligned

The AI must not freestyle based on user examples like 50-200% if the data itself points elsewhere.
